Presentation Notes

ProcureX Evaluation App Explanation

The Evaluation app is the buyer workspace for reviewing submitted bids after a tender has closed. It brings together tender requirements, supplier bid documents, scoring, ranking, recommendation, audit history, and final signed completion.

Evaluation starts after bidding closes. The buyer reviews submitted bids, scores suppliers, selects a recommended bidder, signs the completed evaluation, and the system prepares the recommendation for Awarding and Contracts.

What The Evaluation App Does

  • Shows tenders that are ready for evaluation.
  • Shows saved evaluation drafts and completed evaluation records.
  • Opens a detailed evaluation workspace for one tender.
  • Displays submitted supplier bids, bid receipts, documents, responses, and financial amounts.
  • Loads the evaluation criteria configured during tender creation.
  • Lets the buyer score each supplier and mark one supplier as RECOMMENDED.
  • Requires a digital signature keyphrase to complete the evaluation.
  • Creates or updates an award recommendation so Awards and Contracts can continue the process.

Main User Flow

  1. Buyer signs in and opens /evaluation.
  2. The frontend loads dashboard counts, ready tenders, saved drafts, and records.
  3. The buyer opens a ready tender and reviews submitted bids.
  4. The buyer enters scores, comments, manual review decisions, and ranking.
  5. The buyer selects the recommended supplier.
  6. The buyer completes the evaluation using the signing keyphrase.
  7. The backend stores the completed evaluation and audit trail.
  8. The backend creates an AwardRecommendation.
  9. Awards and Contracts shows the recommendation under award decisions.

How Evaluation Connects To Awarding

When a buyer saves evaluation decisions and one bid is marked RECOMMENDED, the backend creates or updates an AwardRecommendation. Awards and Contracts reads that recommendation and shows it in the buyer queue called awarding-in-progress, displayed as Award Decisions.

The Evaluation app does not stop at scoring. It prepares the official award recommendation record, which becomes the source for the Awarding and Contracts workflow.
